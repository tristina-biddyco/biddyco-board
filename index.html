My side of a delivery: Source → Outreach → Contract → Signed → Ship product → Footage in → hand to design. A date is only as fast as whichever step is still pending — often product in transit, not casting.

📊 The load I'm scheduling against

Standard turnaround is ~4–6 weeks per delivery — but that assumes casting isn't stacked. When this many creators are in motion and being sourced at once, they can't all film in the same window, so the newest asks realistically land closer to 6–8 weeks. That's throughput, not a per-project delay — helpful to factor into what we promise clients.
